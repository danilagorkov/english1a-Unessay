In her commencement speech at Harvard University in 2008 JK Rowling identifies her greatest fear as the fear of failure. In her opinion the fear of failure is comeletely normal as long as it is contained. The fact that you have faced failure means that you can start doing something about it or find people who possess more knowledge than you.

In her TedTalk Barbara Corcoran discusses the difference between the person who tries and fails, and the person who never tries. She uses examples from her life to justify the importance of failure to achieve success in the future.
