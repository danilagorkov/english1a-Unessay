Why Do We Fail?

Information About The Author

Danila Gorkov is a student at Foothill College studying Computer Science for transfer. In addition to analyzing patterns of academic success, Danila wrote a BIAW essay for ENGL 1A that demonstrates a relationship between failure in writing and success in writing. Danila is closely associated with the process of failure, as English is not his primary language. He uses a failure algorithm that allows utilizing failure as a resource for a finer understanding of the subject.

"Do not judge me by my successes; judge me by how many times I fell down and got back up again."

-Nelson Mandela
